# Birthday Story Animation

A 30-second animated story website built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — designed as a birthday gift.

---

## Story Script & Scene Breakdown

### Start Screen
- Dark purple background with a **Start** button.
- Animation does not begin until the user clicks Start.

### Scene 0 — "Once Upon a Time..." (0s – 3s)
- Dark purple background.
- The title text **"Once Upon a Time..."** fades in with a golden glow.

### Scene 1 — The Garden Walk (3s – 10s)
- **Setting:** Sunny garden — blue sky, bright sun, drifting clouds, green hills, colorful flowers, three trees, a garden path.
- A **street lamp** and a **trash can** are visible in the background (trash can is still, no wobble).
- The **couple** (Person 1 in blue, Person 2 in pink) walks across the scene from left to right with bouncing, arm-swinging, leg-walking animation.
- **Narration:** *"On a beautiful sunny day, they went for a walk in the garden..."*

### Scene 2 — The Sound (10s – 17s)
- **Setting:** Same sunny garden with all the same trees, flowers, hills, and clouds.
- The **trash can** near the street lamp is now **wobbling** with **sound waves** and **floating music notes** emanating from it.
- The couple stands nearby with **question marks** bouncing above their heads.
- Arms and legs continue their animated movement.
- **Narration:** *"Suddenly, they heard a strange sound from a trash can..."*

### Scene 3 — The Discovery (17s – 23s)
- **Setting:** Same sunny garden background.
- The **trash can lid is open** (rotated back), and a **golden glow** shines from inside.
- The **baby's face** is revealed inside the can with a rising animation.
- **Sparkles** twinkle across the scene.
- The couple stands on either side of the trash can, peering in.
- The street lamp stands next to the can.
- **Narration:** *"They opened the lid and found... a baby!"*

### Scene 4 — Going Home (23s – 28s)
- **Setting:** Same sunny garden with a **house** on the right side (orange walls, red triangular roof, brown door with gold knob, two lit windows, chimney with rising smoke).
- The couple walks from the left toward the house, with the **baby** carried between them.
- **Hearts** float upward across the scene.
- Arms and legs animate with their walking movement.
- **Narration:** *"They took the little one home and lived happily ever after!"*

### Scene 5 — Finale (28s – 30s)
- Dark purple background with **confetti** raining down.
- **"Happy Birthday!"** pops in with golden text and glow.
- **"This is your origin story ❤"** fades in below.
- All three faces (Person 1, Baby, Person 2) are displayed in golden-bordered circles.
- A **Replay** button appears to restart the animation.
